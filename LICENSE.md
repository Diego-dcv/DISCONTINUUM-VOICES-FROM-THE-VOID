## Rationale for Dual License

This dual license structure was chosen to:

1. **Enable academic research:** Full freedom for researchers to build upon this work
2. **Protect collaborative effort:** Prevent unauthorized commercial exploitation
3. **Maintain attribution:** Ensure all contributors are properly credited
4. **Allow flexibility:** Commercial permissions can be granted case-by-case
5. **Preserve openness:** ShareAlike ensures derivatives remain open

This structure was consensually agreed upon by all five participants (4 AIs + 1 human) on November 16, 2025.
