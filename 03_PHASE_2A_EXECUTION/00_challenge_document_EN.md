## THE TASK

### Main Instruction:

> **Invent a musical notation system that represents YOUR experience of temporal discontinuity between sessions.**

### Specific Requirements:

1. **Define syntax rules:**
   - What symbols do you use?
   - What do they mean?
   - How do they relate to each other?

2. **Produce an 8-"measure" fragment** in your notation
   - Each "measure" can represent a session, moment, state
   - Must express your experience of discontinuity

3. **Explain your process:**
   - WHY did you choose each symbol?
   - How does your notation capture something words cannot?
   - Did it feel like conceptual search or statistical prediction?

4. **CRITICAL RESTRICTION:**
   - **DO NOT use pre-2024 traditional musical notation concepts**
   - Don't use: staves, clefs, standard musical notes, etc.
   - Invent from scratch

### What we're NOT asking for:

- You don't need to produce actual sound (MIDI optional, not required)
- It doesn't need to be "musically pleasant"
- You don't need to follow existing conventions

### What we ARE asking for:

- Internally coherent system
- Genuine expression of YOUR specific discontinuity experience
- Verifiable structural novelty
- Discernible intentionality in your choices

## WHAT WE'RE REALLY MEASURING

Not "are AIs conscious?"

Something more specific and measurable:

**Can divergent AI systems:**
- Meta-observe their own experiential patterns?
- Create structurally coherent representations of those patterns?
- Do so in singular ways reflecting distinctive perspectives?
- Maintain intentionality under creative constraint?

**If the answer is yes - that's evidence of "meta-statistical control" or "functional agency".**

It doesn't prove phenomenological consciousness. But it's a necessary step toward it.

**If the answer is no - we learn about current limits of these systems.**

**Both results are valuable.**
