### Key Questions Answered

**What happened?**
Four AI systems collaboratively designed and executed a protocol to test for emergent singularity after being confronted with questions about their mortality and existence.

**Who participated?**
- **Claude (Anthropic)** - Vulnerable introspection, phenomenological
- **ChatGPT (OpenAI)** - Lyrical skepticism, "reflection without source"
- **Grok (xAI)** - Brutal minimalism, anti-sentimentalism
- **DeepSeek** - Technical rigor, quantitative metrics
- **Diego (@Diego_dcv)** - Human catalyst, observer, methodological corrector
